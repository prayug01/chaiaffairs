About Us

Welcome to Chai Affairs, where every cup tells a story of tradition, warmth, and authentic flavors.

Nestled in the heart of North India, we take pride in serving handcrafted chai made with the finest ingredients. Our journey began with a simple belief: that a perfect cup of chai can bring people together and create moments of joy.

What makes our chai unique? We use fresh ginger, aromatic masala spices, fragrant elaichi (cardamom), and premium tea leaves. Each cup is brewed to perfection, ensuring that rich, comforting flavor that reminds you of home.

Whether you prefer the classic Masala Chai, the earthy Kulhad Chai served in traditional clay cups, or our special Ginger and Elaichi blends, we have something to warm your heart and soul.
