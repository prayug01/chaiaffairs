About Chai Affairs

Traditional roots, modern hangout energy.

We started Chai Affairs to bring the warmth of roadside chai to a calm, minimal space. Every kettle is brewed with whole spices—ginger, masala, elaichi, chocolate, or seasonal blends. Grab a kulhad, plug in, meet a friend, or finish that deadline with the aroma of real chai leaves.

  • Slow brewed in small batches
  • Locally sourced milk & spices
  • Kulhad service & takeaways
